6/20-6/21: ~*.. HUGE Festival ~ JUNE 20th & 21st -- MANY Volunteers Needed :-) (san jose downtown)

St. John

(google map) (yahoo map)

charitable music fest/fair

~*.. We will be in need of **MANY** Volunteers on June 20th & 21st.

Hello there, you fun-loving Silicon Valley folks!

YES, this event is over 7 months away but we need to put our volunteer database & list together RIGHT NOW! -- So please do not wait that long to contact us if you already know that you will be available that weekend ... the sooner the better.

We are the proud sponsors of an annual, historic & national festival that is coming up in June on Father's Day Weekend in Downtown San Jose.

This festival is our annual fundraiser for a local non-profit community services agency; the proceeds are used for assisting very needy children & adults in the area with food, Summer Camp, Holiday toys, etc.

This coming year, we will be in need of a huge amount of volunteers ~ at least 200 (total for both days). ~

Volunteering with us will be the perfect opportunity for high school students to perform their community service hours -- meals & drinks will be provided.

We welcome all retirees, churches, clubs & other non-profit groups to join us for volunteering, as well as to come & enjoy this multi-cultural, multi-faceted and eventful festive celebration!

In addition to general volunteers ages 16 to 39, this year, we are particularly in need of at least 12 older volunteers who are available during the early mornings, fluent in English and have no childcare or transportation concerns ----

---- in this special category, we need adults who:

~.. are at least 40 to 55 (or older),
~.. healthy & energetic with little to no physical restrictions or limitations for being active on your feet,
~.. can volunteer (not less than) 4 or more hours,
~.. and are dependable...can get to the festival on time.

Also in this ' special category ', you will not be doing any lifting or carrying anything -- you will have very light duties. ~~ invite your family & friends to join you and make it even more FUN!!

**.. PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION: The VTA Light Rail will get you right there, as well as VTA bus routes 64, 23 & 22 within a couple of blocks.

All volunteers must be 16 years of age and older. ~ Parents can volunteer with their younger children (ages 10-15), but must supervise them at all times.

Please be very sure that you are available, since this festival always takes place on Father's Day Weekend, so if you have a father you normally spend time with on that day, please be sure to know it you will still be able to help out. Checking your calendar/schedule to ensure your availability would be much appreciated.

There will be a 'volunteer orientation' meeting within 2 weeks before the festival.

Thank you very much for your assistance; looking forward to meeting you & working with you.

Best regards,

Valire Davis for
The 2015 San Jose
'Juneteenth In The Park' Festival
