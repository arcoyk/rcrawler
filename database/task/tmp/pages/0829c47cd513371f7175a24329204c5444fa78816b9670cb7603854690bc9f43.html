12/5-12/7: 24th Annual Santa's Bag Holiday Boutique (lafayette / orinda / moraga)

500 St. Mary's Road

(google map) (yahoo map)

kid friendly sale fest/fair

Beautiful decorations, holiday gifts and the delicious smell of warm baked treats. It's the little things that make this time of year nostalgic. Create new memories at the 24th Annual Santa's Bag Holiday Boutique in Lafayette. Featuring over 60 artists with beautiful handcrafted holiday items and works of art. Gift baskets from local merchants and citizens to benefit the Lafayette Community Center. Get ready for the holidays at the Santa's Bag Boutique Friday 12/5 from 11-7, and Saturday, 12/6 and Sunday, 12/7 from 9-5 at the Lafayette Community Center, 500 St. Mary's Rd, Lafayette. Admission and parking are free.
