Berkeley Underground Events for December

Location: 2284 Shattuck Ave, Berkeley, CA 94704
Webpage: http://www.berkeley-underground.com/

Sunday, December 7th at 9:00pm
Whine Dem Sundays Reggae Night
Join us for Whine Dem Sundays Reggae Nights starting Nov 23rd! REGGAE beats dropping all night!

Wednesday, December 10th, 7:30pm
Salsa Wednesdays
Salsa Wednesdays at Berkeley Underground: Live Bands, Social dancing and Lessons - every Wednesday, 7:30pm - 1:30am. Doors open at 7:30pm. Happy Hour: 8-10pm with Drink Specials! Salsa Lessons with Tomaj Trenda & Tiffany Wu - 7:45-9:15! Live Bands - 9:30pm - 1:30am! Full Bar * Big, Beautiful Dance Floor * Amazing Sound System * V.I.P. Area! Just $15 at the door, ($10 with valid student ID).

Tuesday, December 16th, 9:00pm
What the Funk!?
Free guest list till 11pm $5 after industry free all night. Bast and Bes Ent invite you to WHAT THE FUNK? - a journey into funk music. Groves by Jimmy Hits (super square) & Nacho Vega (Soul Selection Music). Hosted by Sha Boogie. Drink specials / surprise guests / funky give aways. 21+ event.


The Berkeley Underground is San Francisco & the East Bay's newest EDM dance nightclub, featuring touring DJ's, comedy shows, live music and Jazz in a cool NYC Village meets SF speakeasy venue! We are open every week with a calendar of events featuring local, national & international artists. Check out our exclusive bottle service, state of the art sound system and 3 bars! We are located directly below BUILD Pizzeria Roma in Downtown Berkeley, California. The Underground is also available for private parties and corporate events.
