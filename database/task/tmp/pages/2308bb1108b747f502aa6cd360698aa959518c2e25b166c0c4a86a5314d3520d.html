11/22-12/5: The Great Dickens Christmas Fair! (San Francisco, CA)

food/drink fest/fair

In 2014, we celebrate 36 years of bringing to life the dazzling world of Charles Dickens. Limited time only, purchase $3 off discounted tickets up until November 21st! To purchase discounted tickets, visit our website at https://dickensfairtickets2014.eventbrite.com/?access=Craigslist2014Dickens1-3 OR use Promo Code: Craigslist2014Dickens1-3

A beloved Bay Area tradition and one of the most elaborate, inventive and authentically-Victorian Christmas events anywhere in the world, the Great Dickens Christmas Fair & Victorian Holiday Party presents a brilliant facsimile of London circa the 1840s-1860s - a Victorian Christmas Card Come to Life!

Join us
Nov. 22 ~ Dec. 21st, from 10am to 7pm,
including the Friday after Thanksgiving!

Our talented staff, crew, performers, vendors and volunteers are already preparing for this year's event. Once again, over three acres of old-world exhibition halls at San Francisco's legendary Cow Palace will magically transform into a Victorian holiday world of dancing, feasting, entertainments, and shopping for one-of-a-kind treasures! An ever-changing recreated slice of Charles Dickens' London, warmly lit and lovingly decorated, with winding lanes, shops, pubs, tearooms, lecture halls, a grand Music Hall and the London dockside brought to life by more than 750 authentically-dressed actors
