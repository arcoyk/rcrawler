12/7-12/20: Warm Coat Drive for those who are in need (berkeley)

Ecology Center, 2530 San Pablo Avenue

(google map) (yahoo map)

free charitable fitness/health

Warm Coat Drive

Date: November 28th - December 24th
Location: Ecology Center, 2530 San Pablo Avenue, Berkeley, CA, 94702
http://ecologycenter.org/events/warm-coat-drive/

The Ecology Center is partnering with One Warm Coat to host a winter coat drive this holiday season. In the spirit of reuse and giving, bring your worn coats to the Ecology Center November 28th -- December 24th, and put them in our coat bin. We'll be donating our collected coats to local organization Building Opportunities for Self-Sufficiency (BOSS). They serve homeless and recently homeless members of our community, and are very excited about our coat drive!

Please drop off clean, used or new coats and jackets, as well as rain gear, sweaters, and men's socks at the Ecology Center during open hours: Tuesday-Sunday 11am-6pm. Please note two days will have special holiday hours: We will be open one Monday, December 22nd, 11am-6pm; and closing early Wednesday, December 24th, 11am-4pm.
