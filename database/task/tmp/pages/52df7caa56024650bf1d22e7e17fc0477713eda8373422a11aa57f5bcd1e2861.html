12/7-12/20: The Starry Plough Irish Pub Evening Shows, Music, Dance, Open Mic (berkeley)

The Starry Plough, 3101 Shattuck Ave

(google map) (yahoo map)

music food/drink dance

The Starry Plough is an Irish pub, legendary live music venue and kitchen that offers delicious, locally sourced comfort food. With deep roots as an Irish Revolutionary watering hole, the history of protest runs as deep today as it did when we opened four decades ago. The pub is located at 3101 Shattuck Ave. Berkeley.

Among other events, The Starry Plough offers entertainment 7 days a week and feature truly talented, interesting and inspiring artists from the Bay Area and beyond.
Weekend music schedule: http://www.thestarryplough.com/calendar/
Calendar by month (bottom of page): http://www.thestarryplough.com/

Weekly events:
Monday- IRISH DANCE CEILI
Traditional Irish dance and music. Dance lessons at 7pm. Music and dancing at 9pm.

Tuesday - OPEN MIC with Juan Pez
Sign-ups at 7:30pm. Trivia and prizes.

Wednesday - THE BERKELEY SLAM - 8pm.

Thursday - BERKELEY FUNK NIGHT - 9pm.

Friday and Saturday - LIVE MUSIC - 8 pm
Every Friday and Saturday night we present live bands - from local favorites to national / international touring artists. Doors at 8pm/Music at 9pm.

Sunday - All Ages Matinee and Live Music
Every Sunday afternoon we host an all ages matinee featuring everything from local and touring artists to theme based events. Always free & all ages. 4pm-7pm.
Every Sunday evening we present a traditional Irish music session featuring instrumentals & vocals. Players and singers welcomed! Starting at 8pm/no cover. Donations appreciated. (Note: first Sundays Advanced Session is instruments only & invited players).
