Northern California Human Resources Association presents:

Core Competencies for Practicing HR in CA - Part Two: eProgram

Thursday Dec 4, Tuesday Dec 9, Wednesday Dec 10, Thursday Dec 11, Monday Dec 15; 5:00-6:30pm, Online

MORE INFO: http://www.nchra.org/events/event_details.asp?id=443741&group=

This exclusive NCHRA offering specifically geared to the needs of the new California HR practitioner covers the basics of employment law, hiring, compensation, benefits and occupational safety/workers compensation from the unique California perspective. Gain critical knowledge and skills necessary to practice HR in the complex state of California.

December 4 - Welcome, Introduction, and New HR Topics

December 9 - Time Off: Vacations, Paid Time Off, FMLA, CFRA, and PDL Leaves of Absence Integration; PFL and SDI Wage Replacement, Miscellaneous Leaves and Time Off

December 10 - Employment Discrimination and Training Requirements

December 11 - Workplace Safety, Workers Compensation, and other Health and Safety Requirements

December 15 - At-Will Employment, Wrongful Termination, Privacy, and HR Certification Overview
