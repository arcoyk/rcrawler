Hanukah Community Gathering at Berkeley Jewish Community Center

Date: Sunday, December 14th, 3:00pm-6:00pm
Location: Jewish Community Center of the East Bay, Berkeley Branch, 1414 Walnut St., Berkeley, CA 94709

Join us for a community celebration for the whole family featuring music by Isaac Zones, group candle-lighting, storytelling, menorah-making and other arts and crafts projects -- plus latkes! Fair Trade Judaica will offer holiday items for sale. For more information, see http://www.jcceastbay.org/events/2014/12/14/jewishlife/hanukkah-community-gathering/
