12/14: For the Love of Dog USA Holiday Pot Luck Party (SanFrancisco)

1695 Polk St

(google map) (yahoo map)

free charitable food/drink

For the Love of Dog is hosting a pot luck holiday party for you and your dogs at Harper & Rye! Bring your favorite people food Dish to this event to share with others! Meet San Francisco's first Dog Mayor Frida!

Ideas for dishes:
Salads
Vegetable Dishes
Appetizers
Chips and Dip
Dog Treat Trays?

No Drinks of any kind, seeing this is held at a bar that is very dog friendly! Message me to let me know what you will be bringing. You will have to buy drinks from the establishment. Venue can hold up to 150 people!

Please bring some dog food and dog treats to be donated to Bay Area Rescue Groups for the holidays! Be thoughtful and bring a bag of dog food or bag of treats to donate to the cause!

Name:
For the Love of Dog USA Holiday Pot Luck Party

Where:
Harper & Rye
1695 Polk St
San Francisco, CA 94109
b/t Sacramento St & Clay St in Nob Hill

When:
December 14th, 2014
4:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m.

Why:
A socialization event for your dogs and of course people too!

Who:
For the Love of Dog USA - For the Love of Dog USA was formed to help bring awareness to the growing issues surrounding neglected and abandoned dogs. Through social media and online efforts For the Love of Dog USA assists with the challenges of finding forever homes and bringing awareness of neglect and abandonment of dogs throughout the country. Whether it is providing rescue groups with food and supplies in under-served areas or advocating for making shelters and facilities no kill in the United States, For the Love of Dog is there for the dogs.
