12/7-12/20: Takara Sake Tasting and Museum FREE Admission (berkeley)

708 Addison St

(google map) (yahoo map)

free food/drink

SAKE MUSEUM
The Museum features items and displays of interest to both experts and the general public. It includes an exhibit of the historical sake-making process, sake artifacts and implements collected by Takara Sake USA, and a history of sake-making in America. The collection is the only one of its kind in the U.S.A. Admission to the museum is free.

SAKE TASTING ROOM
Our Tasting Room offers a selection of sake tasting flights. Not only can you taste and experience the quality of Sho Chiku Bai Sake, but you can learn about sake production as well. A video describing the story of sake brewing can be enjoyed. Tasting: $5.00 - $10. There are variety of courses of sake tasting to choose from. Most Takara products are available for purchase.

BUSINESS HOURS:　
12:00 pm to 6 pm, Daily

ADDRESS:
Takara Sake USA Inc.
708 Addison St, Berkeley, CA 94710

PARKING:
Street parking is usually available.

For more information visit https://www.takarasake.com/tasting-room.php
