12/6-12/7: SF Bach Choir: A Candlelight Christmas (2515 Fillmore Street)

2515 Fillmore Street

(google map) (yahoo map)

kid friendly music

SF Bach Choir: A Candlelight Christmas
with Renaissance wind band, The Whole Noyse

Saturday, December 6, 8:00 p.m.
Sunday, December 7, 4:00 p.m.
At historic Calvary Presbyterian Church
2515 Fillmore St. (at Jackson St.), San Francisco
http://www.sfbach.org

SFBC transports audiences every year with its beloved candlelight Christmas concert--this time with some new treats and surprises. Our program of seasonal music spans the 15th to 21st centuries, from Dufay, Praetorius, and Lasso to Mendelssohn and Aprahamian, and includes traditional carols as well. SFBC singers will surround you with candlelight to share the joy of this glorious music.

The Whole Noyse joins the choir this year with its eclectic collection of Baroque and Renaissance instruments including cornett, sackbut, curtal, recorder, crumhorn, and shawm, predecessors of modern instruments such as the bassoon, trombone, and oboe. Together they produce a distinctive and gorgeous sound, making the concert nothing short of a grand holiday celebration!

TICKETS:
General Admission: Advance $30/Door $35
Senior (age 62+): Advance $25/Door $30
Students with ID $10
Youth up to age 18 FREE with adult
