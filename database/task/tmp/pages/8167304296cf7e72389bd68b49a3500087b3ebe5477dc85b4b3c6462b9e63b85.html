12/14: 12/14 Free Scottish Holiday Concert (san mateo)

free kid friendly charitable

The Stewart Tartan Pipes and Drums will present their 20th Annual Free Holiday Scottish Concert at a new San Mateo location on Saturday, December 14, 2014, starting at 3:00 PM at Transfiguration Episcopal Church, 3900 Alameda De Las Pulgas, San Mateo, CA.

The concert features bagpipes & drums, Scottish ballads, and special guest appearances by the Peninsula Scottish Fiddlers and the Piedmont Highlanders Dancing School.
Families are welcome (and encouraged to come).
"The band is a relaxed group of musicians, drawn together as much by the camaraderie of the band as our shared interest in the music", said Paul Olson, Drum Major. Current membership is twenty-five, with experience ranging from one year to almost forty years. New pipers and drummers are always welcome. The band practices in Saratoga, CA, on Tuesday evenings. This is the band's third year with a Grade 5 competition band. In 2015 the band will also field a Grade 4, WUSPBA affiliated, competition band

Other performances include a mix of parades, shows, birthdays, weddings and special events, Disney Land, PGA's The President's Cup golf tournament, Silicon Valley Open, SF Giants pre-game, Oakland Raiders pre-game, Davies Symphony, FaceBook and the Mountain Winery with the Chieftains. The Holiday Concert is the highlight of the season.

Donations gratefully accepted.

The Stewart Tartan Pipes and Drums is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit corporation.

Web Site: www.stewarttartan.com
