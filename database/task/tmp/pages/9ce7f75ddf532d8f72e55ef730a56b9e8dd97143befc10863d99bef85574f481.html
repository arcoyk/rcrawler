Live Music Nights, Pizza and Brews at Jupiter

About Jupiter: Jupiter is the East Bay's most popular pizza and beerhouse, locally owned and operated, serving wood-fired pizzas and handcrafted beers ales. Housed in an old livery stable from the 1890's, Jupiter exudes a European atmosphere found in the heart of Berkeley, California.

The food at Jupiter is truly unique, prepared before your eyes at the end of the downstairs bar. Wood-fired pizzas and baked foccacia sandwiches are a perfect compliment to the fresh microbrews, lunch favorites include the open-faced Neptuna Melt sandwich, and the Zeus, Odysseus, and Andromeda pizzas are joined by new (seasonal) pizza recipes year round. The dinner menu expands after five o'clock, with seasonal plates for patrons to share. Everything on the menu is created with Jupiter customers in mind, and each item can be complimented by a Jupiter House Brew. Jupiter is the creation of John Martin, founder of the venerable Triple Rock Brewery in North Berkeley and co-owner of Drake's Brewing in San Leandro. Jupiter will accommodate small to mid-sized groups for your private event, office party, and special occasion. For more information about our music, open hours, and pricing, see our website. See you at the JUP!

Address: Jupiter, 2181 Shattuck Ave, Berkeley, CA 94704
Webpage: http://www.jupiterbeer.com/
Upcoming events for December:

Friday, December 5th, 8:00pm
LES GWAN JUPONS
 Led by SF-based bassist, composer, and musical instigator Lisa Mezzacappa, Les Gwan Jupons play folk and dance music from the French, Spanish and English-speaking Caribbean . . . biguine from Martinique, calypso from Trinidad, cumbia from Colombia, son and bolero from Cuba.

Saturday, December 6th, 8:00pm
TORTOISE & THE PIMPS
 Making their Jup debut, Tortoise and the Pimps has been grooving under the Bay Area radars bigger music scene for over three years. A group of session players from Oakland and San Francisco make up this funky ensemble, as they put a twist on the genres of funk, blues, hip-hop and soul. Don't miss out on a good reason to get up out of your seat and shake it to some real music.

Thursday, December 11th, 8:00pm
Selector: DJ LADY RYAN
 Due to her charm and immeasurable talent, Lady Ryan has easily become a Bay Area favorite, holding residences at the Lucky Lounge, Oasis, and the RockSteady in Oakland. From Jazz to House, the flux of distinctive sounds she so graceful sets beside one another make for a brilliant movement within the crowd.

Friday, December 12th, 8:00pm
RYAN SCOTT LONG
 Oakland drummer Ryan Scott Long has studied drums with some of the Bay's greatest musicians, and currently plays in an array of jazz, funk and hip-hop inspired ensembles throughout the East Bay. "Long comes across as a very intense, musically charged, major drum personality of the future." - Oz Fritz (Grammy Award-Winning Engineer)

Saturday, December 13th, 8:00pm
JOHN LESTER'S BASS BUREAUCRACY
 Bassist/vocalist John Lester brings his new project, Bass Bureaucracy, to Jupiter. John plays upright bass, as well as guitar and six string electric basses, while utilizing loop recording to create deep grooves for his award winning songs. Tonight John is joined by Brazilian drummer/percussionist Celso Alberti.

Thursday, December 18th, 8:00pm
Selector: DJ DELON & DJ CALI
 With the holiday cutting our Selector Thursdays from 4 to 3, DJ Delon welcomes in his Selector-mate DJ Cali of Oakland's the Peoples Party, who's being bumped next week for the Holiday and will come in this week to assist Santa Delon to deck the halls with beats and grooves!

Friday, December 19th, 8:00pm
MO'FONE
 With its surprising and highly combustible line-up of two sax-men and one drummer, the Bay Area-based group MO'FONE has thrilled audiences at home and across the country with some of the funkiest jazz and jazziest funk being made today. Jeremy Steinkoler leads on the traps, delivering a steady onslaught of jazz and funk rhythms. 'Mo'Fone is jazz's answer to rock's power trio' -AllAboutJazz.

Saturday, December 20th, 8:00pm
ERIC VAN JAMES GROUP A Berkeley native, with degrees in music and education, Eric Van James is a pianist, vocalist, composer and was a conductor and music theory instructor for the Kairos Youth Choir, as well as a former faculty member of the Jazzschool. Tonights quartet will include Ken Stout on alto & soprano sax, Rod Verette on electric bass, and Robi Bean on drums.

Friday, December 26th, 8:00pm
SAM PRIVEN TRIO
 Sam Priven is a 20 year old composer and saxophonist from Albany, California. Sam has had the honor of receiving an outstanding soloist award at Lincoln Center from Jimmy Heath and Wynton Marsalis, as well as being the recipient of numerous downbeat awards in both small and large ensemble settings. He was also selected for the lead alto-chair in California's high school all state jazz band. Sam is an alum of the Jazzschool and is currently studying jazz at the University of Miami Frost School of Music and is making his Jup debut tonight!

Saturday, December 27th, 8:00pm
JONAH UDALL QUARTET
 Jonah Udall is an eclectic, probing guitarist, composer and band leader based in Miami and the San Francisco Bay Area. His musical interests and talents however extend far beyond the small jazz ensembles he most often performs in. He also composes works for classical chamber ensembles and large jazz ensembles, and enjoys playing world music styles on guitar, mandolin and other stringed instruments ranging from Afro-Cuban to Brazilian Choro to Balkan.

Wednesday, December 31st, 9:00pm
New Year's Eve Blowout with BANDULU
 Come say farewell to the old funky smelly year and ring in the shiny New Year at Jupiter, with party favors and a champagne toast, all to the grooving sounds of Oaklands own Bandulu! Actually making their Jup debut, it was in the East wing of Berkeley's historic Cloyne Court Hotel and Casino where Bandulu founding members, David and Alan, discovered their affinity for golden-era Funk, Soul, and Reggae music. David, 20 at the time, and Alan, 18, pooled their last $60 for a used drum set and turned their room into a rehearsal space. Musicians came and went, but the sessions resulted in a prolific amount of material and a stint in the local underground. Some years later in Oakland, California, David and Alan re-connected with some old friends, stumbled upon some new friends, and the eclectic funk group Bandulu was officially born. Come see the last of 2014 and the beginning of 2015 with your peeps at Jupiter!
