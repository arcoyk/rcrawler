12/7-12/20: The New Parkway Theater Activities for the Week (oakland lake merritt / grand)

The New Parkway Theater, 474 24th St.

(google map) (yahoo map)

art/film free food/drink

The New Parkway Theater offers evening activities, Monday thru Thursday nights, nearly all free, so please come in and join us for good food, good drink, and a good dose of community:

OPEN MIC MONDAY
Is live performance, combating stage fright, or just plain talking to strangers part of your new year's resolution? Well come on by and share your creative side at the New Parkway Open Mic Night! All forms of expression are welcome, be they music, poetry, spoken word, comedy, or dance. Come out and bring your skills or just sit and support the brave performers. Open mic happens every 1st and 3rd Monday of the month. Sign up starts at 7:30, show at 8:00pm.

TASTY TUESDAY
Tasty Tuesday is our way of celebrating Oakland's thriving local food and beverage scene. Tasty Tuesdays will include pop-ups from local eateries, craft beer and wine tastings, special food-centric activity shares, and much more! Stay tuned about upcoming events when the series starts this spring.

DRINK & DRAW WEDNESDAY
Unleash your creativity during Drink & Draw Wednesdays. From 7:30 onwards, we've got arts and crafts upstairs on the mezzanine, and down at the bar we have plenty of local libations to fuel your creative flame. Quite fitting for the city that boasts the largest percentage of artists anywhere in the US.

THRIVIA THURSDAY
From 7:30-9PM on Thursday nights, we do team trivia with three rounds of fascinating questions that will make you the life of the next party. Come with a team or come by yourself and join up with others. No great wisdom or trivia experience necessary. This is a good time out amongst friends. And the winning team gets a pitcher of beer or a handful of free movie tokens.

ALL WEEK LONG
Every night is game night here at the New Parkway. Come check out our collection of new and old school board games up on the mezzanine. Bring your friends and relive your childhood with Guess Who or Monopoly, or start training for the next local chess or scrabble tournament. We will occasionally have local game experts come out to introduce new games and/or community game spaces.


Location: 474 24th St., Oakland.
For more information see http://thenewparkway.com/wp/?page_id=20
