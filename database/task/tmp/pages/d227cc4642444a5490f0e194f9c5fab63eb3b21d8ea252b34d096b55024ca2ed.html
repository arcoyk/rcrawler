12/11-12/20: Oakland's Art Murmur FREE ART and GALLERIES TOUR Sat. Dec. 20th 2-4pm (oakland downtown)

Joyce Gordon Gallery, 406 14th Street

(google map) (yahoo map)

art/film free

Saturday Stroll

Led by Oakland curators, writers, and artists. Each tour brings visitors to 3-5 galleries, with short presentations from curators or artists at each location.

SATURDAY | DECEMBER 20th | THEME: ART FOR THE HOLIDAYS

Led by Eric Murphy of Joyce Gordon Gallery.
Meet at Joyce Gordon Gallery, 406 14th St., Oakland, CA 94612, 2:00pm.
District: Downtown
Featuring a special family art making activity at the Museum of Children's Arts!

SATURDAY | JANUARY 17th | THEME: VIDEO ART

Led by Conrad Meyers of Aggregate Space Gallery.
Meet at Aggregate Space Gallery, 801 W Grand Ave (enter on West), 2:00pm.
District: West / Uptown

For more information visit http://oaklandartmurmur.org/saturdaystroll
Map of galleries: http://oaklandartmurmur.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/oam-venue-map.pdf
