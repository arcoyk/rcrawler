Events at Jack London Square

Location: Jack London Square, Broadway and Embarcadero, Oakland, CA 94607
Weekly Events: http://www.jacklondonsquare.com/events/weekly-events
Special Events: http://www.jacklondonsquare.com/events/special-events


Dockside Tours of the Presidential Yacht Potomac
Every Wednesday, Friday, and Sunday | 11:00am-3:00pm
Enjoy a docent led tour of the Presidential Yacht, FDR's Floating White House. Tours last approximately one hour. http://www.yelp.com/biz/uss-potomac-oakland

History Cruise on the Presidential Yacht Potomac
Friday - Sunday | 10:30 AM-1:30 PM
Cruise into history on board on the USS Potomac; enjoy a docent led tour of FDR's Floating White House while underway and enjoy a fully narrated cruise around San Francisco Bay.

Holiday Carolers
Weekdays December 8th to 23rd | 6:15pm-7:45pm
Enjoy TOSCA, the Oakland Symphony's chorus group while visiting Jack London Square. This ensemble of beautiful voices will be singing holiday carols live and in person on the beautiful waterfront.

Donations Accepted for the Holidays
Throughout the Holiday Season 9:00am-5:00pm
Donations will be accepted for the: Alameda County Community Food Bank - non perishable food items are welcomed, Oakland Fire Department's Random Acts of Kindness Toy Drive - unwrapped toys for all ages.

Sunday Seafood Suppers
Every Sunday at Jack's Oyster Bar & Fish House (336 Water Street)
Jack's Oyster Bar & Fish House, the second restaurant concept of owners and industry veterans Rick Hackett and Meredith Melville of Bocanova restaurant in Jack London Square, is a waterfront restaurant that overlooks the harbor, the perfect setting for slurping oysters and feasting on the sea's bounty. Every Sunday, Chef/Owner Rick Hackett and Executive Chef Peter Villegas will offer a Sunday Seafood Supper menu, where guests can enjoy a prix-fixe three-course seafood menu for $34.00 per person. The menu allows guests the option to choose between two dishes per course and will change every week. Reservations are recommended.
